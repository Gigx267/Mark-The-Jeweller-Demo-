The jeweller

Christopher Nwosu

Third-generation lapidary artist, GIA graduate gemologist, and visionary behind the brand. Christopher began his journey as an apprentice in Antwerp before returning to Nigeria to create a maison that rejects compromise.

“Luxury isn't loud — it’s felt in the weight of a perfectly balanced ring, the warmth of rare rose gold, and the story a gem tells when held to light.” — Mark Christopher

Today, he personally oversees each bespoke commission, from wax carving to final polish, ensuring every creation carries the house signature: a discrete ‘MC’ cipher and a hidden ruby inside each shank — a nod to intuition and passion.
